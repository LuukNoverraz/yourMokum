Nieuw-West

Nieuw-West is a sprawling, high-density suburban residential area in the west of Amsterdam. It is the most populous borough at over 150,000 inhabitants.

Zuid

Zuid is one of the most densely populated boroughs with 8,500 homes per square kilometer. It is home to the Museum Quarter and the Zuidas business district.

West

Amsterdam-West was built between roughly the end of the 19th century and 1960. It is the most densely populated borough of Amsterdam and hosts a variety of neighbourhoods and cultures.

Oost

Marked by bordering the Singelgracht, Amstel river and IJ lake, Oost is a diverse and colorful borough that is also home to the HvA.

Noord

Noord is seperated from the other boroughs of Amsterdam by the IJ lake, and is perfect for those who want to see a part of Amsterdam less crowded.

Zuidoost

Home to the Johan Cruyff Arena and surrounding entertainment and shopping district, Zuidoost is a multicultural borough that is an exclave of Amsterdam.

Centrum

Centrum is the historical city centre of Amsterdam and home to its most recognizable landmarks. For a classic Amsterdam experience, Centrum offers limitless possibilities.

Westpoort

Westpoort covers the port of Amsterdam, which is the main harbour and industrial area of the city. It is the corporate headquarters to over 1500 companies.
