yourMokum

Make Amsterdam yours.

Broaden your horizons.

If you've just come to Amsterdam, the amount of things to see and do might be overwhelming. With its rich history and international influences, there are a hundred ways you could go.

With yourMokum, you can make Amsterdam yours. Every borough has its own web where community contributions can show you what to do. Take a walk, go by bike, discover new parts of the city and make Mokum yours.
